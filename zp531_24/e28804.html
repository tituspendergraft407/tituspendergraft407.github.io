It is characterized by the two General Electric turbofan engines and the adoption of one of the most powerful aircraft gun ever flown: the 30 mm. GAU-8/A Avenger Gatling. Thanks to its eleven underwing and under fuselage pillars the A-10 can carry an impressive offensive payload. Includes decals for 4 versions. Skill Level 3.
