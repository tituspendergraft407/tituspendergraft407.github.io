Fukushima Fiction: The Literary Landscape of Japan's Triple Disaster

著者名: Rachel DiNitto 出版者: University of Hawai"i Press 出版年: 30 May 2019 版表示: シリーズ: HardbackIntroduces readers to the literary works that have emerged out of Japan"s triple disaster, now known as 3/11. The book provides a nuanced picture of the varied literary responses to this ongoing tragedy, focusing on "serious fiction", the one area of Japanese cultural production that has consistently addressed the disaster and its aftermath.
